In this map the states are coloured on a scale depending on their meat import in US$ in 2015. Darker colours represent higher values of meat import. When hovered, the exact import value becomes clear. Next to the country that highlights when hovered, their corresponding bar in the top barchart highlights as well, to put their value in perspective. When a bar in the top barchart is being hovered, their corresponding country highlights as well in the map When to countries are clicked, a grouped barchart is made under the map, showing the growth of each country in the years 2011 to 2015. Note that this function is not yet fully operational since the same barchart is made twice.
